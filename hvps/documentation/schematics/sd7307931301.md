## Description

Output filter capacitor circuit including ripple filter from transformer secondary 105% tap.

```
  Filter Capacitor Details:
  - Capacitance: 8 uF per stage (4 stages total)
  - Fed from 105% voltage tap on transformer secondary
  - Series resistance: 2 x 500 ohm = 1000 ohm
  - Purpose: Ripple reduction on HVPS output

  Output Capacitor:
  - 0.22 uF across full output
  - Stored energy at 90 kV: 911 J

  Normal Discharge (with klystron load): < 1 second
  Abnormal (output open): ~160 seconds to < 50V
  Internal failure (caps isolated): ~81.5 minutes
```
