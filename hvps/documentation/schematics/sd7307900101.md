## Key Component Details

### Phase-Shifting Transformer
- Rating: 3.5 MVA (assumed, spec says 350 kVA but likely typo)
- Configuration: Extended delta
- Creates two 3-phase waveforms: +15° and -15° shifted
- Output: 12.91 kV RMS phase-to-phase

### Rectifier Transformers T1, T2
- Each rated: 1.5 MVA
- Type: Open wye primary
- T1: Two secondaries (T1-S1, T1-S2) at 21.0 kV RMS
- T2: T2-S1 at 21.0 kV RMS, T2-S2 at 10.5 kV RMS

### Thyristor Phase Control Stacks
- 6 stacks per open wye primary (12 total)
- Each stack: 14 Powerex T8K7 thyristors in series
- Snubber per thyristor: 0.047 uF capacitor
- Stack snubber: 0.015 uF capacitor (R-C series)

### Filter Section
- 2 inductors: L = 0.3 H each, rated 85 A full load
- 4 filter capacitors: 8 uF each (from 105% tap of secondary)
- Series resistors: 2 x 500 ohm per capacitor circuit
- Output capacitor: 0.22 uF across output

### Crowbar Protection
- 6 thyristors per crowbar stack
- 5 Mohm voltage sharing resistors per thyristor
- Total stack resistance: 30 Mohm

### Voltage Dividers (not shown in schematic)
- 2 identical dividers across output
- Total resistance: 100 Mohm each
- Bottom 10 kohm measures 9.1 V at 91 kV output
